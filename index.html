About Me

My name is Rachael Hodson:

I am an introvert, lifelong learner, problem solver, and general creative type eager to combine these superpowers into a career in tech. I love the idea of marrying the cold logic of code to the playfulness of design to make client websites that are both interesting and useful.

When I'm not at my computer, you'll find me with my family — alternately laughing to the point of tears (my kids are hilarious) or muttering vague curses about socks left on the floor (is it that hard to find a hamper?). I'm addicted to Dr Pepper and true crime documentaries. I adore and am adored by my neurotic rescue mutt, Smores.
